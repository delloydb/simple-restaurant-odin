Our Journey

How It All Began

2010

Humble Beginnings

Founded by Chef Marco in a cozy 20-seat space, Foodites started as a passion project with just three employees and a dream to revolutionize local dining.

2015

First Michelin Star

Recognized for our innovative farm-to-table approach and signature tasting menu. This put us on the culinary map as a destination restaurant.

2018

Cookbook Published

Released "The Foodites Philosophy," sharing our culinary secrets and farm-to-table recipes with home cooks worldwide.

2023

Expanded Location

Moved to our current flagship location in downtown, featuring an open kitchen, chef's table, and rooftop garden supplying fresh ingredients.
